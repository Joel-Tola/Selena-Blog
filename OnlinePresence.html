Why Selena Gomez is a 'Rare' Celebrity on Social Media?

Numerous celebrities populate social media, but only a handful effectively navigate the digital landscape. Selena Gomez stands out as a prime example of a celebrity who excels in fostering engagement and delivering diverse content on her Instagram platform. Evidently, her social media prowess is reflected in her impressive following - once the most-followed person on Instagram in 2016, she currently holds the sixth position with a substantial 297 million followers.

What sets her apart?

Gomez strategically utilizes her platform, skillfully sharing personal photos, narratives, product promotions, announcements related to her "Rare" beauty line, and actively championing various charitable causes and foundations. While many celebrities focus on showcasing their affluent lifestyles or promoting products, Selena goes beyond the superficial, eschewing mere displays of opulence, like luxury cars or exotic vacations, and instead contributes meaningful content to her audience.

Make it Personal

The significance of infusing a personal touch into your content lies in the desire of your followers to connect with the genuine individual behind the online persona, especially in the realm of celebrity. Demonstrating that your significant influencers share relatable aspects of their lives reinforces the human connection. This concept is integral to personal branding, the art of managing and presenting oneself to the public. As aptly stated by Lee (2015), "You're a brand. I'm a brand. We're all brands, whether we aim to be or not" (para. 5). Even if you're marketing a tangible product, the primary focus remains on selling your authentic self. By embracing your true identity, you not only inspire your followers to do the same but also foster engagement with your posts. Selena Gomez exemplifies this approach by sharing glimpses of her life, such as photos with her dog or a simple greeting like "Hi," prompting hundreds of followers to respond in kind.

Promotions

As mentioned earlier, while you may be marketing a tangible product, followers are likely to become disenchanted if your content primarily revolves around sales pitches. They crave exclusive peeks into new products, special offers, discounts, and content that holds value for them. Selena Gomez excels in striking a balance by seamlessly integrating a variety of personal and promotional content.

Engaging effectively with your audience on social media requires no secret formula, but a crucial component is embracing 'The 80/20 Rule' in your strategy, as advocated by De Beule (2013): allocate only 20% of your content to brand promotion and reserve the remaining 80% for material that genuinely captivates your audience, fostering meaningful conversations. Selena adeptly adheres to this principle by not only promoting her "Rare Beauty" line and other ventures but also delivering content that resonates with her audience, evident in increased likes, comments, and shares.
