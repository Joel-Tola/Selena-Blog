Rare Beauty

Rare Beauty" is a cosmetics brand founded by Selena Gomez. Launched in September 2020, the brand has garnered attention for its commitment to inclusivity, positive messaging, and efforts to support mental health initiatives. Here are key aspects of Rare Beauty:

Inclusive Beauty Standards:

Rare Beauty is dedicated to promoting diverse and inclusive beauty standards. The brand offers a wide range of makeup products, including foundation, concealer, blush, and lip products, with a focus on catering to various skin tones.

Positive Messaging:

Selena Gomez has emphasized the importance of embracing one's uniqueness. The brand encourages individuals to celebrate their individuality and express themselves through makeup, fostering a positive and empowering message.

Mental Health Initiatives:

One distinctive feature of Rare Beauty is its commitment to supporting mental health initiatives. A percentage of Rare Beauty's sales goes toward organizations working to address mental health issues and improve access to mental health resources.

What makes us Rare

Together we’re building a safe, welcoming space in beauty and beyond. This is makeup made to feel good in, without hiding what makes you unique. And it’s all vegan and cruelty free.

Product Range:

The product range includes a variety of makeup items designed to enhance natural features. Rare Beauty's formulations are known for being lightweight, versatile, and suitable for different skin types.

Accessible Beauty:

Rare Beauty aims to make beauty accessible to all individuals, regardless of age, gender, or background. The brand's emphasis on inclusivity extends to its marketing and product development.

Retail Availability:

Initially available online and through Sephora, Rare Beauty has expanded its retail presence to make its products more widely accessible to consumers. Selena Gomez's vision for Rare Beauty goes beyond traditional beauty standards, seeking to create a space where individuals can feel empowered and confident in their unique qualities. The brand's emphasis on mental health awareness aligns with Gomez's broader advocacy efforts in this area. Rare Beauty has received positive recognition for its impact on the beauty industry and its commitment to social responsibility.
