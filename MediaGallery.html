Ask Questions

What better way to increase engagement on a post then by asking a question? Selena often asks questions in her captions, which results in many people responding on her posts. This allows her to a. give them the content they want to see, b. understand how they are responding to different types of content, and c. makes her followers feel like she is listening to them. Selena does so in the above image by asking, “did you guess right about who killed Tom Kono?” People responded with their guesses. This is a fun game for her followers, promotes Selena's show, and allows for a great deal of engagement on her post.
